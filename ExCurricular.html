Extra Curricular

Previous Next

G.A.A

Our school has a strong tradition of G.A.A for both boys and girls. We are involved in hurling, camogie and Gaelic football. Most of our students also play with our local G.A.A club.

Swimming

1st to 6th class students take part in swimming lessons each year. Lessons take take place in the APEX leisure centre.

Choir

Students from 3rd to 6th class are involved in the school choir.

Irish Dancing

This year Irish Dancing has started in the school hall every Tuesday. Junior & Senior Infants takes place from 2-3pm and 1st to 6th class from 3-4pm

Library

Our 1st to 6th class students visit New Ross Library weekly prior to their swimming lessons. The library has a fantastic selection of books for all ages and they organise many events for children every month.

Other

Each year we organise one off events such as drumming, hip hop dancing, outings to the theatre, spelling bee and much more. We are also working towards achieving the Active Schools Flag.
