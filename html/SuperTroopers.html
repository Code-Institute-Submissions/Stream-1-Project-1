Super Troopers

Super Troopers is associated with Laya Healthcare. It is the first health homework programme ever to be run in Ireland. It encourages children and their families to be more active and learn about healthy lifestyles. ​

The Programme includes short burst, fun activities around physical activity, wellbeing and nutrition. It promotes both a healthy mind and healthy body. ​

SuperValu is the official healthy eating partner to Super Troopers and this year our 2nd and 3rd class students visited Supervalu in Thomastown where they met with a nutritionist to learn all about healthy eating and the food pyramid. They also got to sample some delicious fruits,  breads and meat! ​

At Scoil Mhólainge we promote a healthy eating policy for all our students. All students must bring healthy lunches. No sweets or crisps are allowed. Monday, Tuesdays and Wednesdays are sugar free. Thursday and Friday students are allowed to bring one small bar. We remind parents to be aware of the high sugar content in some types of yoghurt snacks and fruit based drinks. We encourage all students to include fruit and vegetables in their lunches. ​

Along with healthy eating we encourage all our students to be more active and this year we have committed to Active Schools and we hope to have an Active School Flag shortly."
