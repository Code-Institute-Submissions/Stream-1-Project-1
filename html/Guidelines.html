Guidelines

1. School Times

  • School starts at 9.20am
  • Junior and Senior Infants finish at 2pm
  • 1st - 6th Class finish at 3pm

For the month of September Junior Infants finish at 12.30pm as it is important for the pupils to settle into their new environment.

Please Note:
The school doors open at 9.20am. Where children are dropped before this time parents / guardians must be aware that there is no supervision prior to this time. The school is responsible for children during school hours only.

2. Lunch times & healthy eating

  • First break is at 11am.
  • Lunch is from 12.30 - 1pm.

We promote a healthy eating policy for all our students. All students must bring healthy lunches. No sweets or crisps are allowed. Monday, Tuesdays and Wednesdays are sugar free. Thursday and Friday students are allowed to bring one small bar. We remind parents to be aware of the high sugar content in some types of yoghurt snacks and fruit based drinks. We encourage all students to include fruit and vegetables in their lunches.

For some healthy lunch ideas check out the following websites:

  • www.safefood.eu/Education/Primary-(ROI)/Healthy-lunchboxes.aspx
  • https://www.hse.ie/eng/services/Publications/Children/Healthy_Lunch_Box_to_Grow,_Learn_and_Play_Part_1.html

5. Uniform

The Uniform consist of:

  • Navy Jumper/Cardigan
  • Light blue shirt
  • Grey trousers / pinafore / skirt
  • Red tie

As there is no crest on our school jumpers our uniforms can be purchased locally at Kennedy Draperies New Ross or at other stores such as Penny’s, Dunnes Stores, Marks and Spencers, Debenhams, Next, Tesco’s etc.

The school tracksuit is worn on P.E days which consist of a:

  • Navy Hoodie (this can only be bought through the school, parents/guardians will get notification by Anne O’Sullivan in May/June in relation to this)
  • Plain navy tracksuit bottoms – no lines or designs

Please label all pupils clothes and belongings with their names.

3. Dropping and Collecting

As a safety measure when dropping or collecting children from school parents/guardians are asked to enter and exit the school yard through the bottom gate.

4. Parking

Parking spaces are limited. Please do not park in the teachers car park or in the bus bay. We encourage all Parents / Guardians to be extra careful when walking their children to and from the car and when manoeuvring their cars.

6. Booklist

The book list will be made available and posted to all students during June/July.

7. Transportation

Bus Eireann operates a school transport scheme on behalf of the Dept. Of Education. Applications for school transport can be made online at www.buseireann.ie or forms can be downloaded and submitted to your local Bus Eireann office.
