Parents Association

As a parent / guardian of a child enrolled in our school you are an automatic member of our Parents Association. Your ideas and support are important in helping to make the Parents Association in our school a success.

​ The Parents Association meet every 8-10 weeks during the school year. Each year we organise meetings , talks, social events and fundraisers. These are a means of getting to know new people, while at the same time contribute greatly to the social life of the school. ​

The Parents Association are always keen to have new members on the committee. Please contact our Chairperson Ray Shannon or our Secretary Anne Sullivan if you wish to find  out more.

National Parents Council Primary

We are members of the NPC which exists to ensure that all parents are supported and empowered to become effective parents in their child's education. They provide parents with information leaflets, training courses, a parent helpline(01) 8874477 and so much more.  ​

Currently they are running two online courses that any parent can do from home    

  • Internet Safety
  • Supporting your child to build healthy friendships and relationships

​ To find out more about the National Parents Council Primary please visit their website www.npc.ie. ​
